Join Us

This conference is aimed at developers, operators and users to discuss and evolve the open source software project, its functionality and real world operability. Part talks, part workshops, part hackathon, this event will present a great opportunity for attendees and sponsors alike.

The talks and events at the conference will provide ample opportunity to meet with the CloudStack community and engage in deep discussions with developers and users. The hackathon day will provide an excellent opportunity to turn ideas into practice together with the committers and the sponsors. Watch our video to experience CloudStack Collaboration Summit.

The hackathon will have a significant focus on Continuous Integration, testing and working through questions around GitHub pull requests, however all participation is welcome!

Montreal is active and beautiful at this time of year, so we recommend planning an extra day to take advantage of this amazing city.
